Concepts and Definitions

Outlier Definition

"An observation which deviates so much from other observations as to arouse suspicions that it was generated by a different mechanism."

Outlier Meaning

What is the meaning of a given outlier? For that meaning, what would we do with that outlier?

  1. Meaning: Unwanted data. Aim: Data cleaning.
  2. Meaning: Event of interest. Aim: Analyze the outlier.
