Standard Physics Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) considers PDEs of the following form:

$$
  u_t + \mathbf{N}[u ; \lambda] = 0
$$

$$
  u_t + \mathbb{N}[u ; \lambda] = 0
$$

$$
  u_t + \mathbcal{N}[u ; \lambda] = 0
$$
